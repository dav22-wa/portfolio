My Journey

My path into digital consulting and technology began with a simple belief: technology should empower everyone, not just the tech-savvy few. Growing up in Kenya, I witnessed firsthand how digital literacy gaps limited opportunities for talented individuals and businesses.

This realization drove me to pursue Computer Science at the University of Embu, where I didn't just learn to code—I learned to solve problems. During my studies, I started volunteering to teach basic computer skills to youth in my community. Seeing their faces light up when they created their first email account or built their first website ignited a passion that would shape my career.

My professional journey truly began when I started freelancing, helping small businesses establish their online presence. Each project taught me that technology alone isn't the answer—it's about understanding business goals, user needs, and creating solutions that bridge both worlds. This led me to expand my services into digital marketing, business consulting, and comprehensive training programs.

A pivotal moment came when I won the Mozilla Responsible Computing Challenge 2024 for developing an AI-powered solution that helps farmers detect crop diseases. This project embodied everything I stand for: using technology to solve real-world problems and create tangible impact in underserved communities.

Today, I work with individuals, startups, established businesses, and NGOs, helping them navigate the digital landscape. Whether it's crafting a marketing strategy that drives growth, building a website that converts visitors into customers, or training teams to work more effectively with digital tools, my approach remains the same: strategic thinking, practical solutions, and measurable results.

But beyond the professional achievements, what drives me most is the opportunity to empower the next generation. Through my digital literacy programs, I've trained hundreds of young people, many of whom have gone on to build their own tech careers or start digital businesses. This ripple effect—creating opportunities that create more opportunities—is what gets me out of bed every morning.

I believe we're living in the most exciting time in human history. The digital revolution has democratized access to information, markets, and opportunities like never before. My mission is to help as many people and organizations as possible harness this potential, turning digital challenges into competitive advantages.

This is just the beginning. The best is yet to come.
